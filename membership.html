Membership Information

Note: Our financial year ends 31st March - please inquire about part year subs.

Adult Membership

Posted 10 Jan 2014

An adult member is a person aged between 16 and 60 years on the 1st January.

Cost: $40 year.

Senior Membership

Posted 10 Jan 2014

A senior member is aged over 60 years on 1st January.

Cost: $35 year.

Junior Membership

Posted 10 Jan 2014

Junior members are aged less than 16 years on the 1st January.

Cost: $15 year.

Junior members do not have voting rights at the Annual General Meeting (AGM) or any Special General Meeting.
Junior members may not hold the positions of President, Vice-President or Secretary/Treasurer on the Council.

Family Membership

Posted 10 Jan 2014

Family Membership may be applied to members of the same immediate family group or whanau.  The subscription will not exceed 75% of the total for same number of individuals.


If you would like to join:

Sorry, we don't have the ability to join you up online.  You can grab a copy of our Membership Application by clicking the link under Downloads in the sidebar.

Print the form out and fill it in.  Then post it to us OR email us the details.


If you have any other questions:

Please contact us via the email links in the Contacts section of the sidebar.
